Methods

Pipeline Overview

The pipeline follows a standard genome assembly and annotation workflow:

  1. Quality Control: NanoPlot for read quality assessment
  2. Adapter Trimming: Porechop removes adapters
  3. Read Filtering: Chopper filters short/low-quality reads
  4. Assembly: Flye (--meta mode) for metagenomic assembly
  5. Polishing: Medaka corrects sequencing errors
  6. Assembly QC: QUAST evaluates assembly metrics
  7. Gene Prediction: PHANOTATE predicts phage genes
  8. Annotation: BLAST against viral and bacterial databases
